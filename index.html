Energy Saving Tips

Use Natural Light

Maximize daylight to reduce artificial lighting needs. Open curtains and blinds during the day.

Potential Savings: 5-10%

Optimize Thermostat

Set your thermostat 1-2°C lower in winter and higher in summer to save energy.

Potential Savings: 3% per degree

Unplug Devices

Unplug chargers and electronics when not in use to eliminate phantom energy drain.

Potential Savings: 5-10%

AC Maintenance

Clean filters monthly and ensure proper insulation to improve AC efficiency.

Potential Savings: 10-15%

LED Lighting

Replace incandescent bulbs with LEDs which use 75% less energy and last longer.

Potential Savings: 80% per bulb

Use Fans Wisely

Ceiling fans allow you to raise thermostat setting about 4°F with no reduction in comfort.

Potential Savings: 10-20%
